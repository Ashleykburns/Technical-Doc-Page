Phalaenopsis Orchid Care
  • Watering
  • Sunlight
  • Temperature
  • Fertilizer
  • Repotting
Watering

once a week, in the morning

  • When the roots turn silver in color & the potting medium is dry
  • Soak the roots & meduim for 10 mins in clean, room temperature water
  • Let pot drain for 15 mins
  • Never use ice

Sunlight

bright, indirect light

  • Place in a room that gets sun most of the day
  • Do not let the sun hit the plant at any time
  • Mid to high height placement is best

Temperature

70 °F with humidity

  • No drafts, avoid cold
  • Lowest temp: 65°F (night) / Highest temp: 80°F (day)
  • A humidifier is a great option for dry locations

Fertilizer

every six weeks

  • Use a granular fertilizer that inclues macronutrients and trace elements
  • Dilute fertilizer per label instructions
  • Use mixture to water plant as usuall

Repotting

once per year

  • The best time to re-pot is just after flowering, or when new growth appears
  • Use a potting mix specifically for orchids, bark chips are best
  • Repot sonner if: yellow foliage, lack of growth, or damaged roots
